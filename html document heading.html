android 12

Android 12 is the twelfth major release and 19th version of Android, the mobile operating system developed by the Open Handset Alliance led by Google.
The first beta was released on May 18, 2021. Android 12 was released publicly on October 4, 2021,
through Android Open Source Project (AOSP) and was released to supported Google Pixel devices on October 19, 2021.

android 13

Android 13 is the thirteenth major release and the 20th version of Android, the mobile operating system
developed by the Open Handset Alliance led by Google.
It was released to the public and the Android Open Source Project (AOSP) on August 15, 2022.
The first devices to ship with Android 13 were the Pixel 7 and 7 Pro.
